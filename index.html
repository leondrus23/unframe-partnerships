Play 01

Customer-to-Channel Conversion + GSI Blueprint Packaging

Diagnosis: Cushman & Wakefield and Avison Young aren't just case studies — both are global real estate services firms with advisory client bases of their own. No public evidence either has been asked to sell Unframe forward. Separately: the one named SI relationship, Intelagen (a boutique Google Cloud Partner, April 2025), proves the concept at small scale — there's still no relationship with a top-tier GSI, independently flagged as the top structural risk to growth.

Motion: Two tracks, different speeds. Near-term: convert Cushman & Wakefield / Avison Young from customers into resellers — faster than cold-starting a relationship with a top-tier GSI. Medium-term: package 2–3 proven Blueprints as a white-label methodology GSIs can certify consultants on.

Play 03

The Pricing/Procurement Tension Behind the Open Question

Diagnosis: No AWS or GCP Marketplace listing could be independently confirmed — unconfirmed, not asserted either way. But "no upfront cost," Unframe's own pricing story, is structurally the opposite of how enterprise cloud-commit drawdown works. Unframe's proven buyers (an F500 bank, real estate services firms) are exactly the profile most likely to prefer routing spend through committed cloud credits.

Motion: Confirm the real status in week one. If no listing exists, it moves from nice-to-have to critical-path before the tension becomes a closing blocker at the next contract size up.
